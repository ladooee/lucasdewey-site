  - title: Junction box air leak redesign
    summary: Diagnosed and corrected an air leak caused by seal material interaction, localized plastic deformation, and air ingress past the dial interface.
    contribution:
      - Investigated the interaction between silicone grease, silicone sealing material, and molded plastic deformation
      - Redesigned the seal using an alternate material better suited to the application
      - Added a center cutout to reduce force on the plastic wall while preserving sealing function
      - Used prototype testing to confirm that the seal change also reduced dial removal force, avoiding an unnecessary tooling change
    result: Improved sealing reliability while reducing tooling risk and simplifying the corrective action path.
